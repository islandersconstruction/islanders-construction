Our Story

Quality Residential Renovations Across Hawaii

Crafting Beautiful, Functional Spaces in Hawaii

Welcome to Islanders Construction. We are a locally owned and operated remodeling company specializing in high-quality kitchen and bathroom renovations. From modern kitchen upgrades to spa-like bathroom transformations, we bring exceptional craftsmanship and close attention to detail to every home we touch.

Our mission is simple: to transform your existing space into a beautiful, functional environment that fits your family's lifestyle. We manage your project with clear communication, honest timelines, and a commitment to durable, stunning results that stand the test of time.
